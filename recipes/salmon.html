Salmon

Description

An Asian inspired marinade for baked salmon.

Ingredients

  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 1 lb Salmon
  • 1 tsp Red chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp Grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 3 tbspSoy sauce
  • 1 tspSesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/2 lemon juice

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the salmon together and pour into a ziplock bag
  2. Place the salmon into the ziplock bag
  3. Massage the marinade into the salmon until fully coated and rest in the fridge for 30 mins
  4. Flip over and rest for another 30 mins
  5. Bake in the oven at 350 for 10-15 mins
