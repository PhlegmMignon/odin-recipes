Italian bread dip

Description

Italian bread dip with oregano, parmesan, black peper, garlic, and olive oil

Ingredients

  • Half pint of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 2 cups grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  1. Grate the garlic into a bowl
  2. Mix all the other ingredients into that same bowl
  3. Wait 6 hours for the garlic to lose its sting
