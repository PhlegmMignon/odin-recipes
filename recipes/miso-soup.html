Miso soup recipe

Description

A classic Japanese soup

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls bonito flakes
  • 200 grams kombu
  • Half brick of tofu
  • 5 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 tbsp yellow miso

Steps

  1. Soak shiitake mushrooms and kombu in 1 liter water for 16 hours
  2. Remove shiitake and kombu and bring the water to just below simmer
  3. Soak bonito flakes in the near simmering water for 5 mins
  4. Cut shiitake and tofu into bite sized chunks
  5. Strain out the bonito flakes and discard
  6. In near simmering water, boil the tofu and shiitake for 10 mins
  7. Dissolve miso into water and serve
